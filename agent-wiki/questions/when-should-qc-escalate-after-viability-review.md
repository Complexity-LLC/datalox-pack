# When should QC escalate after viability review?

## Question

When does an ambiguous viability-gate decision exceed normal operator discretion and require escalation?

## Answer

Escalate when the proposed gate change would materially affect downstream interpretation, QC acceptance, or release readiness.

## Escalate When

- The sample would flip from pass to fail.
- The ambiguity is not explained by a documented exception.
- The operator cannot defend the choice from visible evidence alone.
